Leon are a women’s football team based in East London - collectively we’ve been playing together since 2016. Some of us had never even kicked a ball when we started so we’ve come a long way!

Leons are fierce and determined creatures. They may appear to be innocently smelling a beautiful flower, but beware they are ready to strike at a moment’s notice.
